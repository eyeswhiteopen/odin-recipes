Lasagna

Description

A pasta type meat dish.

Ingredients

  • 200g minced beef
  • Pasta sheet
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Mix minced beef with tomato sauce.
  2. Lay the mix in step 1 above over a layer of pasta sheet.
  3. Place another layer of pasta sheet above that.
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 until desired height.
